Reach of the path
A long solid ribbon means openpilot is sure of the road well ahead. As confidence drops the ribbon dissolves and pulls back toward the car — you see trust shortening before anything goes wrong.
Edge strain
In a hard curve the strained edge of the path brightens as openpilot nears its steering-torque limit — an early, directional cue instead of an alert after the wheel gives out.
Hand-off
Only when a hand-off is likely within seconds does a calm edge-breath and a plain prompt appear. Sound is reserved for this moment, so it means something.
Note. Independent concept for the comma design challenge — not affiliated with comma.ai. Road scene is stylized; the interaction model (living path, edge strain, reserved escalation) is the proposal.
