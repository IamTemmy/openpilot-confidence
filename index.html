Reach of the path
A long solid ribbon means openpilot is sure of the road well ahead. As confidence drops the ribbon dissolves and pulls back toward the car — you see trust shorten before anything goes wrong.
Edge strain
In a hard curve the strained edge of the path brightens as openpilot nears its steering-torque limit — an early, directional cue instead of an alert after the wheel gives out.
Hand-off + sound
Only when a hand-off is likely does a calm edge-breath, a plain prompt, and a soft reserved chime appear. Silence means trust — so the sound, when it comes, actually means something.
Note. Independent concept for the comma design challenge — not affiliated with comma.ai. The camera view is a stylized render, not real footage; the proposal is the interaction model (living path, edge strain, reserved escalation) — which is designed to sit on the real camera feed unchanged.
